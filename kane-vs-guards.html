GAMEPLAY SCRIPT – CHAPTER THIRTY-FIVE

Location: Guard Wing Entrance · Time: 17 July, 13:10 · Player: Kane

Objective: Reach Maria before it’s too late

CUTSCENE

CAMERA: Close-up on the trembling wooden door.

KANE (VO):
The old wooden door shakes and creaks as I push it open...

[CAMERA ROTATES – Kane slips in through a narrow gap]

KANE (VO):
With the smallest of openings, I slither myself inside, like a snake on a mission to hunt for food.

GAMEPLAY BEGINS

QTE TRIGGER:
[Enemy Attack Incoming] → [Press RT to Dodge | Miss: Take 15% damage]

IF FAIL:
KANE (VO): My right cheek aches, eyes watering slightly...
[Blood splashes – CAMERA SLO-MO IMPACT]

DIALOGUE MOMENT

Guard punches Kane in the face. Kane retaliates.

PLAYER PROMPT:

[A] Strike back instantly
[B] Ask who sent him
[C] Step back and observe

[A] Strike Back (Canon Path)

Kane punches the guard. Crunch. The guard howls and calls reinforcements.

GUARD: “BROTHERS, THE PRINCE IS HERE.”

Morality Impact: –10 (Aggression)
Rage Meter: +15%
Skill XP Gained: Flame Mastery +5

[B] Ask Who Sent Him

KANE: “Who told you to hit me?”
GUARD (panicking): “Everyone! The king! You—you weren’t supposed to make it here!”

Morality Impact: +5 (Diplomacy)
Rage Meter: +5%
Skill XP Gained: Diplomacy +10

[C] Step Back and Observe

Kane says nothing. The guard hesitates, then calls out to the others.

Morality Impact: +0
Rage Meter: +0
Skill XP Gained: Stealth +5

CUTSCENE CONTINUES

GUARD (chuckling nervously): Backing away, rubbing his neck

KANE: “I can clearly see you walking away.”

GUARD: “Uhh, you can?”

KANE: “Are you alright?”
“Of course I can.”

GUARD: “You broke my one good feature, of course I am not alright.”

KANE: “Sorry about that, but you did land the first punch.”

GUARD: “I did, but only because I was ordered to keep you away from the princess.”

MORALITY CHECKPOINT: “Who do you fight for?”

PLAYER CHOICE:

[A] “She’s not yours to keep from me.”
[B] “So you’re another pawn in his game?”
[C] “Stand down. I won’t say it again.”

VFX: Fire begins to flicker from Kane’s fingertips.

KANE (VO): The darker my thoughts become, the hotter my fingers become...

FLAME TIER TRIGGER UNLOCKED: “Volcanic Blade”

Skill Unlocked: Volcanic Blade – Unleash a fiery strike that cleaves through multiple enemies. Powered by Rage. Costs 30% Rage Meter. Cooldown: 15s.

GUARD: “What are you do—”

Blade at his throat.

GUARD: “Please let me go.”

KANE: “I really do not have enough time for this...”

OBJECTIVE UPDATE

New Objective: Let the guard live or kill him

PLAYER CHOICE:

[A] Spare him — “Get up. Walk away. Never look back.”
[B] Kill him — “Wrong answer.”

[A] Spare Him
Guard stumbles away.
Morality Impact: +10 (Mercy)
Rage Meter: –10%
Skill XP Gained: Diplomacy +15

[B] Kill Him
Kane slices through him. Blood spatters.
Morality Impact: –20 (Execution)
Rage Meter: +20%
Skill XP Gained: Flame Mastery +10

CUTSCENE — GUARD WAVE ENTERS

SFX: Marching boots, door slamming shut

KANE: “I will give you two choices...”

SFX: Swords drawn

KANE: “I see how it is.”

COMBAT SEQUENCE: "Trial by Fire"

Enemy Count: 5

Combat System:

  • Flame Blade (Heavy Damage)
  • Breakable Enemy Swords
  • Melt/Chop Kill Styles
  • Environmental Damage (Torches, Oil)

PLAYER PROMPTS:

Press [Y] for flame slash Press [LT + RT] to parry-break Press [B] to dodge roll Press [L3] to trigger Flame Burst (AoE)

FINISHERS TRIGGERED BY:
Hitting specific combos / Critical Rage (60%+)

CUTSCENE: STANDOFF

Remaining enemies circle. Breathing heavy. Fire fades.

KANE (raising fists): “Come on, fight me.”

BRAWL MODE

Combat Style: Bare-knuckle

Trigger: 2 enemies grab Kane while 3 punch

[Tap A] to resist hold [LT] to deflect gut punch [RT] to activate Blade Surge escape

FINAL COMBAT: EXECUTION FINISH

KANE (VO): I lift my shoulder blade, then twist my body...

Multiple guards drop in a single fiery strike. Blood paints the walls.

Blade retracts. Kane runs.

OBJECTIVE UPDATE

Reach the Chamber

VFX: Tunnel blurs. Heartbeat audio intensifies.

KANE (VO): My heart is leaping out of my chest...
