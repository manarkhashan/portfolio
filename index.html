About Me

I’m a 24 year-old published author with a deep passion for storytelling in all its forms. My debut poetry collection, Ghost Poetry, explores raw emotion and the human experience.

Currently, I’m working on building my C# and Unity skills. Writing and making my own games as experiments. I am also currently writing my first fantasy novel, called Anguish.

I love crafting characters that feel real, flawed, layered, and alive.

I also published my first ever tell-tale game called Scattered. I designed it fully using Twine and Unity. I designed the game all on my own, from the story itself, branching choices, and the 2D animations.

Scattered is a surreal, choice driven story told through falling dreams, shifting worlds, and strange creatures. Is is like a dream that’s trying to tell you something you can’t quite understand. And it shouldn't take more than 10-20 mintues playthrough.
